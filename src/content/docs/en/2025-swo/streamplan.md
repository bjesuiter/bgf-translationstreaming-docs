## Technical Notes

### Before Each Community Hour

1. Check which languages should be active (see table above)
2. Activate the corresponding channels in the streaming software
3. Test audio connection with translators
4. Ensure translators are ready

### During Community Hour

- Monitor audio levels of all active channels
- Pay attention to participant feedback
- Maintain contact with translators

### After Community Hour

- Deactivate channels
- Give translators a break
- Prepare for the next community hour
